## Decisions

- Implement two separate filters for provider options and settings fields to allow full modularity
- Store field configs in global `$awm_field_configs` registry for masking and encryption
- Use `EWP_Encryption::is_encrypted()` to prevent double encryption of already-encrypted values
- Use `EWP_Encryption::is_masked()` to detect masked placeholder values and skip re-encryption
